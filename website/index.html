Core Features

Everything a Kathak Student Needs

From first steps to advanced Chakkars — Ghoongroo adapts to every level of your classical dance journey.

🎯

Grace Score Engine

An AI scoring system using Cosine Similarity, Bone Vector Normalization, and Beat Synchronization. Measures posture accuracy, step alignment, and timing precision — expressed as a single Grace Score out of 100.

🤸

Real-Time Pose Detection

Powered by Apple Vision framework, the camera tracks 19 body landmarks in real time — spine, shoulders, arms, hips, knees, and ankles — giving instant posture overlays as you dance.

🥁

Interactive Taal Engine

A precision beat system with visual Sam, Taali, and Khaali markers. Practice to Teental (16 beats), Jhaptal (10 beats), or Ektaal (12 beats) with audio bols synchronized to your movements.

💡

Insight Engine

Per-cycle analysis that detects rushing arms, missed Sam beats, posture instability, and graceful moments — delivering feedback in plain language so you can improve each taal cycle.

📚

Structured Learning Path

Step-by-step lessons for every element — from beginner Namaskar and Thaat to advanced Chakkar spins. Each element has detailed visual guidance with costume context.

🔄

Chakkar Spin Detection

The engine detects Chakkar spins through shoulder-crossing patterns in 2D pose data, awarding a 5-point bonus per detected spin — rewarding advanced technique automatically.

Rhythmic Cycles

Practice with Authentic Taals

Every taal is mapped beat-by-beat with Sam, Taali, and Khaali markers — so you always know where you are in the cycle.

16 Beats · 4+4+4+4

Teental

The most common taal in Hindustani music. A 16-beat cycle in four equal vibhaags — the foundation every Kathak student learns first.

Dha Dhin Dhin Dha | Dha Dhin Dhin Dha | Dha Tin Tin Ta | Ta Dhin Dhin Dha
10 Beats · 2+3+2+3

Jhaptal

An asymmetric 10-beat cycle (2+3+2+3) with a distinctive lilting rhythm. Commonly used in semi-classical Kathak compositions.

Dhi Na | Dhi Dhi Na | Ti Na | Dhi Dhi Na
12 Beats · 2+2+2+2+2+2

Ektaal

A meditative 12-beat cycle of six pairs. Often performed in Vilambit (slow) laya, Ektaal allows elaborate improvisation and nuanced expression.

Dhin Dhin | DhaGe TiRaKiTa | Tu Na | Kat Ta | DhaGe TiRaKiTa | Dhi Na
